Atmosphere

Concept

Just steps from Sakae Station, STELLA is a hidden bar where you can take your time enjoying shisha and fine drinks.


In a refined, intimate space, savour the fragrance of shisha, quality cocktails, and moments of pure relaxation.


Whether it's a nightcap, a second stop, or simply an evening to reset — this is a grown-up escape from everyday life.

"A sanctuary for adults
who seek refined pleasures
of shisha and premium spirits."

First-timers are always welcome.
Our staff will guide you to the perfect flavour.
Drinks-only visits are perfectly fine.
Come alone or with friends — all are welcome.
